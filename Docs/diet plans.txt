Diet Plan for Adult Men (25-50 years old)

Breakfast:

    Whole grain toast with avocado spread
    Scrambled eggs
    Greek yogurt with berries
    Green tea or black coffee

Mid-Morning Snack:

    Handful of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews)
    Apple or banana

Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
    Mixed green salad with olive oil and balsamic vinegar dressing

Afternoon Snack:

    Hummus with carrot and cucumber sticks
    Whole grain crackers

Dinner:

    Baked salmon
    Sweet potato

Diet Plan for Adult Women (25-50 years old)

Breakfast:

    Overnight oats with almond milk, chia seeds, and berries
    Hard-boiled egg
    Green tea or herbal tea

Mid-Morning Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey and sliced almonds
    Orange or kiwi fruit

Lunch:

    Quinoa salad with mixed vegetables (bell peppers, cucumber, cherry tomatoes)
    Grilled tofu or chickpeas
    Light vinaigrette dressing
    Whole grain pita bread

Afternoon Snack:

    Sliced cucumber and bell pepper with hummus
    Whole grain rice cakes

Dinner:

    Stir-fried tofu or tempeh with mixed vegetables (broccoli, snow peas, carrots)
    Brown rice or cauliflower rice
    Steamed edamame

Evening Snack (Optional):

    Low-fat Greek yogurt with sliced strawberries

Diet Plan for Seniors (65+ years old)

Breakfast:

    Oatmeal with low-fat milk, chopped nuts, and sliced banana
    Soft-boiled egg
    Decaffeinated tea or coffee

Mid-Morning Snack:

    Cottage cheese with peach slices
    Whole grain crackers

Lunch:

    Lentil soup with whole grain bread
    Baked chicken breast
    Steamed green beans
    Garden salad with light vinaigrette dressing

Afternoon Snack:

    Apple slices with almond butter
    Herbal tea or decaffeinated coffee

Dinner:

    Baked fish (salmon or tilapia)
    Mashed sweet potatoes
    Steamed broccoli
    Whole grain dinner roll

Evening Snack (Optional):

    Sugar-free yogurt with mixed berries

These diet plans are designed to provide balanced nutrition while considering the specific dietary needs and preferences of different age groups and genders. Adjust portion sizes and food choices according to individual requirements and consult with a healthcare professional or registered dietitian for personalized advice.
